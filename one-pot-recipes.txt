Meatballs:
  Ground beef 1lb
  1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  1/2 cup onion diced
  1 egg
  1/2 T salt
  1/2 T pepper

Pasta:
  4 cups egg noodles
  2 cups milk
  2 cups beef broth
  1 cup parmesan
  1/2 cup parsley

1. Mix the beef, breadcrumbs, onion, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl. 
2. Heat up dutch oven with olive oil. Form the meat mix into meatballs. Once heated, brown the meatballs for 1 minute on each side.
3. Pour the beef broth and milk in. Bring to a boil and then add the egg noodles. Cook for the time on the package of the egg noodles, stirring regularly.
4. Add parmesan and parsley
